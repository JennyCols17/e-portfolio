Background of the Training
Project Involvement

Project Overview

During my internship at Chuan Lam Auto Supply, I built a brand-new official website from the ground up using Node.js and Firebase to replace a compromised legacy system. To prevent a repeat of previous security breaches, I implemented a multi-layered defense strategy, including Firebase Security Rules for granular data access control, JSON Web Token (JWT) authentication, and Environment Variables (.env) to protect sensitive API keys. I also integrated CORS policy and input sanitization within the Node.js backend to mitigate XSS and unauthorized cross-origin requests. This secure architecture not only restored the company’s digital presence but also provided a stable platform for the Encoding Department to finalize orders and sync inventory in real-time without the risk of data leaks.

Project Objectives

• Security Restoration and Data Protection: The primary goal was to deploy a brand-new web infrastructure using Node.js and Firebase to eliminate previous vulnerabilities. By implementing Firebase Security Rules, Environment Variable (.env) protection, and input sanitization, the objective was to ensure that the company’s internal data and digital presence are shielded from unauthorized access and future breaches.

• Modernization of the Digital Catalog and Contact System: To build a fast, responsive, and professional website from scratch that serves as a reliable digital showcase. This objective aligns with the company’s mission to provide an accessible platform where customers can view the automotive product catalog and easily contact the company, ensuring 24/7 visibility without technical downtime.

• Optimization of Administrative Data Finalization: To develop a specialized backend interface that assists the Encoding Department in the systematic ending or closing of order records. The goal was to replace manual data entry with a streamlined digital tool, ensuring that finalized transactions are accurately logged and that the company’s internal records are consistently up-to-date.

• Real-Time Data Integrity and Inventory Accuracy: By utilizing Firebase’s real-time synchronization, the objective was to ensure that any updates made by the encoding team are instantly reflected across the company's internal system. This ensures that the product catalog remains accurate, preventing discrepancies between the physical stock in Tondo and the digital records displayed on the website.

However, my OJT at Chuan Lam Auto Supply pushed these skills further by requiring me to build a live production system from scratch. Summary of
OJT Reflection

Challenges & Successes

My biggest challenge was rebuilding the company’s website following a security breach while managing high-volume order encoding. I initially struggled to balance the urgent development of a secure **Node.js** backend with the high-pressure demand for data accuracy, but I overcame this by implementing a systematic workflow and **Firebase** security middleware. My greatest success was delivering a platform that not only restored the company’s digital presence but also streamlined the "ending of orders" process. By automating manual tasks and hardening system security, I provided a meaningful contribution that significantly improved the department’s daily operational efficiency and data integrity.

Personal Growth

I started this OJT as a student uncertain of my real-world capabilities, but I finished as a confident developer who successfully restored a compromised system. Facing a security crisis head-on helped me embrace my identity as a future IT professional, teaching me that I can handle high-pressure environments. I evolved from someone who simply "wrote code" into a professional who understands the weight of responsibility and the impact of technical decisions on a business.

Key Takeaways

The most vital lesson I learned is that security and data integrity are the foundations of any business, especially in a fast-paced industry like auto supply. I realized that technical skills are only half the battle; professional behavior, such as accountability in "ending orders" and clear communication with management, is equally essential. Moving forward, I will always prioritize a "security-first" mindset and maintain a high standard of accuracy in every technical task I undertake.

Moving Forward

This experience deepened my understanding of Information Technology by showing me how code acts as the backbone of physical operations. I plan to carry these lessons into my remaining academic requirements and future job applications, showcasing my ability to manage both development and operations. My goal is to leverage my hands-on experience with Node.js and Firebase to pursue roles that challenge me to build resilient systems that solve real-world business problems.
