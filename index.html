Background of the Training
Project Involvement

Project Overview

During my internship at Chuan Lam Auto Supply, I built a brand-new official website from the ground up using Node.js and Firebase to replace a compromised legacy system. To prevent a repeat of previous security breaches, I implemented a multi-layered defense strategy, including Firebase Security Rules for granular data access control, JSON Web Token (JWT) authentication, and Environment Variables (.env) to protect sensitive API keys. I also integrated CORS policy and input sanitization within the Node.js backend to mitigate XSS and unauthorized cross-origin requests. This secure architecture not only restored the company’s digital presence but also provided a stable platform for the Encoding Department to finalize orders and sync inventory in real-time without the risk of data leaks.

Project Objectives

• Security Restoration and Data Protection: The primary goal was to deploy a brand-new web infrastructure using Node.js and Firebase to eliminate previous vulnerabilities. By implementing Firebase Security Rules, Environment Variable (.env) protection, and input sanitization, the objective was to ensure that the company’s internal data and digital presence are shielded from unauthorized access and future breaches.

• Modernization of the Digital Catalog and Contact System: To build a fast, responsive, and professional website from scratch that serves as a reliable digital showcase. This objective aligns with the company’s mission to provide an accessible platform where customers can view the automotive product catalog and easily contact the company, ensuring 24/7 visibility without technical downtime.

• Optimization of Administrative Data Finalization: To develop a specialized backend interface that assists the Encoding Department in the systematic ending or closing of order records. The goal was to replace manual data entry with a streamlined digital tool, ensuring that finalized transactions are accurately logged and that the company’s internal records are consistently up-to-date.

• Real-Time Data Integrity and Inventory Accuracy: By utilizing Firebase’s real-time synchronization, the objective was to ensure that any updates made by the encoding team are instantly reflected across the company's internal system. This ensures that the product catalog remains accurate, preventing discrepancies between the physical stock in Tondo and the digital records displayed on the website.

Discipline-Related Solutions
Integrated into the Company

Security Hardening & Access Control (Firebase + JWT)

Challenge: The company previously experienced a security breach, which highlighted risks such as unauthorized access, weak request validation, and the potential exposure of internal records.

Solution: Using my BSIT background in cybersecurity concepts and web development, I implemented layered protection: Firebase Security Rules for controlled access, JWT-based authentication for protected actions, environment variables to keep sensitive keys out of the codebase, and backend input sanitization + CORS policy to reduce common web attack vectors.

Integration: These controls were applied directly to the Node.js backend and Firebase configuration, then tested using real workflow scenarios (normal user vs. restricted access). The result was a safer foundation that supported daily operations without exposing internal data.

Encoding Accuracy & Documentation Workflow (Process Improvement)

Challenge: The Encoding Department handled a high volume of orders and paperwork daily. With repetitive manual encoding, small mistakes (wrong item, quantity, spelling, or missing details) could cause delays and extra back-and-forth during order finalization.

Solution: Using my BSIT training in systems thinking and quality control, I introduced a consistent personal workflow: a quick checklist for required fields, a standardized format for order references, and a verification step by cross-checking encoded details against the source documents before marking an order as complete.

Integration: This approach was applied in my daily encoding tasks and document processing. It improved consistency in how orders were encoded and helped reduce rework during reviews, especially on busy days with many transactions.

Responsive Website UI/UX (Professional Digital Presence)

Challenge: The company needed a modern, professional website that could be accessed easily on mobile devices and still look clean and trustworthy for customers and stakeholders.

Solution: I used UI/UX principles from my coursework to design a clear information structure, consistent typography, and responsive layout behavior. I improved navigation and readability so key sections could be found quickly, supporting both usability and credibility.

Integration: The UI/UX improvements were implemented directly in the final website build and aligned with real user needs (quick access to information, easy navigation, and consistent design). This helped strengthen the company’s online visibility and made the site easier to maintain.
