I am Jenny Mae A. Colo, a fourth-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at the Technological Institute of the Philippines (TIP), Manila. With a strong foundation in software development, systems analysis, and network administration, I have consistently applied theoretical knowledge to practical, real-world scenarios throughout my academic journey.

My academic background has equipped me with skills in programming languages such as Java, Python, and PHP, as well as experience in database management, web development, and UI/UX principles. I thrive in collaborative environments and am passionate about leveraging technology to create meaningful, efficient solutions that address genuine user needs.

Looking ahead, I aspire to build a career at the intersection of software engineering and systems design — contributing to organizations that value innovation and continuous improvement. My OJT experience has been a pivotal step in bridging academic learning with professional practice, and I am excited to bring both discipline and creativity to every challenge I encounter.
